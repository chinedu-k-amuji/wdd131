Who We Are

Nedu Phone Accessories Limited is committed to providing high-quality phone accessories and delivering exceptional customer service. Our journey began in August 2018, and we've since grown into a trusted name in Nigeria’s mobile accessories market. We offer products such as phone chargers, data cables, AirPods, power banks, headsets, pouches, microphones, and more.

Our commitment is to supply durable and top-tier accessories that enhance customer satisfaction. We serve both retail and wholesale markets, ensuring you get the best quality.
