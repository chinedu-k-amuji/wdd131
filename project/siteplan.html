Site Name

Nedu Phone Accessories Limited was chosen because it clearly conveys our mission of providing phone accessories solutions to our customers. It ensures branding and visibility. More so, being my business, I used the last four letters(Nedu) of my name "Chinedu" to named the business.

Optional domain availability: neduphoneaccessories.com

Site Purpose

This website will showcase our range of phone accessories products, provide an easy-to-navigate platform for customers to make inquiries, buy our products, and keep users informed about the latest products and promotions.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the best protective phone cases for my device?
  • What are the latest charging accessories available?
  • Can I get reliable and lasting phone battery from your company?
  • Hope your company's phone screens are original?

Color Schema

  • Blue (#007bff): Used for headings and call-to-action buttons.
  • Light Gray (#f8f9fa): Background color for an approachable look.

Typography

  • Arial: Used for body text for readability.
  • Georgia: Optional decorative font for special headers.

Wireframe

The following wireframe represents the home page layout for both mobile and desktop views:

  • Mobile View: Header -> Navigation Menu -> Featured Products -> Footer
  • Desktop View: Header (with logo) -> Horizontal Navigation -> Sidebar for Categories -> Featured Products -> Footer
Wireframe Design Here’s a simple wireframe for both mobile and desktop views. Mobile View: - Header (Full Width):- Logo and business name. - Navigation menu in a collapsible "hamburger" icon. - Hero Section:- A banner with an image or background color. - A tagline (e.g., "Your one-stop solution for phone accessories"). - Call-to-action (CTA) buttons like “Shop Now” or “Contact Us.” - Featured Products:- A scrolling carousel displaying the top-selling or new arrivals. - About Section:- Short text about Nedu Phone Accessories Limited. - Contact Form:- Name, email, subject, and message input fields. - Footer:- Links to Privacy Policy, Social Media icons, and Address. Desktop View: - Header (Horizontal Layout):- Logo on the top-left. - Navigation links (e.g., Home, Shop, About, Contact) displayed horizontally. - Hero Section:- Large banner with images/text on the left and a CTA button on the right. - Sidebar (Left):- Categories like Chargers, Cases, Cables, Screen Protectors. - Main Section (Center):- Display featured products grid (3 or 4 per row). - Footer (Full Width):- Social media links, newsletter subscription, and quick links.
